MsgGuard Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-06-13 · privacy@msgguard.app

Summary: MsgGuard filters spam SMS on your device by default. We do not upload message bodies unless you subscribe to Pro and enable Cloud AI.

What we collect

DataDefaultPurpose
SMS bodyNot collectedOnly if Pro + Cloud AI on
Filter labels / feedbackOn-device / hashImprove accuracy
Analytics (event names)YesProduct metrics
PurchasesVia ApplePro entitlement

On-device processing

Rules, Bayes, and Core ML models run locally. No SMS content leaves your phone in the default configuration.

Cloud AI (opt-in)

Pro users may enable Cloud AI in Settings. Single messages are sent over HTTPS to api.msgguard.app for classification only. Disable anytime.

Retention

Server feedback ~90 days; analytics aggregated 30–90 days; crash reports 30 days if opted in.

Your rights

Contact privacy@msgguard.app for access or deletion requests.
